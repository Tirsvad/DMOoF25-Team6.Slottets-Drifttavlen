## Executive Summary
Slottets Drifttavlen is a digital system designed to optimize workflows and information sharing in care and nursing environments. The system will replace paper-based routines and ensure efficient communication, task management, and documentation.

## Problem Statement
Paper-based processes and manual routines lead to inefficiency, risk of errors, and lack of overview. There is a need for a digital solution that can support daily tasks, ensure data integrity, and facilitate information exchange.

## Objectives
- Digitize workflows and task management
- Improve information sharing between shifts
- Ensure GDPR compliance and data security
- Enable future expansion and integration

## Options and Alternatives (0-scenario, 1-scenario, 2-scenario)
- 0-scenario: Continue with current paper-based routines
- 1-scenario: Implement a simple digital system with basic functions
- 2-scenario: Develop a scalable, cloud-ready system with integration and reporting

## Benefits
- Increased efficiency and overview
- Reduced risk of errors
- Better documentation and traceability
- Improved work environment and user satisfaction

## Costs
- Development costs (software, infrastructure)
- Training and implementation
- Ongoing maintenance and support
